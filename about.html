About Alegre Solutions GS

We didn't learn the trades.
We worked them.

Every system we sell, we built first - in our own home service business, with our own money on the line.

From job sites to operating three home service companies

The owner of Alegre Solutions GS started as a commercial electrician - blue collar, hands-on, working job sites long before "marketing agency" was ever part of the plan.

He quit that job and started a window cleaning business from scratch. No marketing playbook, no agency on retainer - just figuring it out one job at a time. That business grew, and so did the hunger to scale further.

He went on to acquire and operate three home service businesses across different markets, learning through trial and error what actually moves revenue in the trades: not more leads, but a system. Speed-to-lead. Consistent follow-up. A CRM that tracked everything. A sales process that closed.

Once that system was in place inside his own businesses, growth stopped being a guessing game. That's the system Alegre Solutions GS now installs for other contractors.

Why "operators," not "marketers"

Most agencies sell traffic and walk away. We've sat in the truck wondering why the phone wasn't ringing. We've watched a great lead go cold because follow-up was three hours too slow. That difference shapes everything we build.

Why everything is "done for you"

Contractors don't have spare hours to learn ad platforms or babysit a CRM. We built our systems to run themselves - because that's what we needed when we were running job sites ourselves.
