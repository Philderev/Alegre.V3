What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function and collect information about how they're used.

Categories of Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Required for core site functionality (navigation, security). Always active.
  • Analytics: Help us understand site traffic and usage patterns. Only active if you accept them.
  • Marketing: Used for ad performance tracking and retargeting (e.g., Meta Pixel, Google Ads tags). Only active if you accept them.

Your Choices

When you first visit the Site, our cookie banner lets you Accept All, Decline Non-Essential, or open Cookie Settings to choose categories individually. Your choice is stored in your browser's local storage and you can change it anytime using the "Cookie Settings" link in the site footer.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use, such as advertising platforms and our CRM provider. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie practices.
