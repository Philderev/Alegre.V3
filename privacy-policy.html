Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Overview

Alegre Solutions GS ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through alegresolutionsgs.com, how we use it, and the choices available to you, including rights recognized under Nevada's online privacy law (NRS 603A) and general US consumer-privacy practice.

Information We Collect

We may collect: (a) information you provide directly, such as your name, phone number, email address, business name, and trade, when you submit a contact form, request a Growth Assessment, or call us; (b) information collected automatically, such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and referring URLs, typically through cookies and similar technologies; and (c) information from third-party platforms (such as Meta or Google) when you interact with our ads or content on those platforms.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to: respond to inquiries and provide requested services; operate, maintain, and improve our website and marketing systems; send SMS, email, or phone communications related to your inquiry or our services (see our SMS Disclosure in the footer of every page); measure and improve advertising performance; and comply with legal obligations.

SMS & Phone Communications

If you provide a phone number, you consent to receive calls and text messages from Alegre Solutions GS related to your inquiry, our services, and account updates. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out of SMS at any time, or HELP for assistance. Consent to receive texts is not a condition of purchasing any service.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential, analytics, and marketing cookies as described in our Cookie Policy. Analytics and marketing cookies are not activated until you accept them through our cookie banner.

Your Choices - Nevada Residents (NRS 603A)

Nevada law allows consumers to submit a request directing an operator not to sell certain covered information collected about them. Alegre Solutions GS does not sell personal information for monetary consideration. If you would like to submit a verified request under NRS 603A regardless, visit our Do Not Sell My Info page or our Data Request page.

Data Sharing

We share information with service providers who help us operate our business (such as our CRM platform, hosting provider, and advertising platforms), and as required by law. We do not sell personal information to third parties for monetary consideration.

Data Retention & Security

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal obligations, and we use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are directed to business owners and are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at info@alegresolutionsgs.com.
